Music Mind
Project

What is this ?

MusicMind is a tool that generates a short music track based on your personality. It works by first asking you a few questions about yourself to get a brief insight into your characteristics. Based on this information it will generate a music snippet that suits you. But what can you use it for? Maybe you just want to show it to your friends or use it as a ring tone, but if you want to you can even use it for your own projects! It‘s totally up to you!

About

The idea of MusicMind first came up when our team brainstormed for new project ideas, it was amongst five or six others. But in the end the combination of music, AI and social interactions was made MusicMind most intriguing for us. At first it all started pretty small by just designing some UI elements and informing ourselves about different ways to interpret a user’s personality. Soon after that we started planning the whole thing out and, since we all are still students, will continuously update and improve the product.
